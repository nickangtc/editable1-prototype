Word-level editor prototype

Click in the grey box. Arrow around, use Shift+arrows to multi-select, then hit Backspace or Delete.

Known bugs:

  • Right key navigating when range selected is not working as expected. Should go right from end of range, not start of range.
  • Down/up key navigating when holding Shift is not working as expected. It highlights everything. This is likely hard to solve as it depends on viewport width.
